04 — Why tarot
  • Is tarot fortune-telling? +
    Not really. Tarot is a tool for reflection. It surfaces patterns, asks difficult questions, and helps you think through decisions. It doesn't predict your future; it helps you understand your present.
  • Do I need to believe in it? +
    No. Sceptics often find it more useful because they engage with the prompts critically. The cards are a mirror, not a mandate.
  • What should I come prepared with? +
    A question, or even just a feeling. You don't need to have it figured out. That's what the session is for.
  • Are sessions confidential? +
    Completely. What you share in a session stays there. Nothing is recorded, shared, or referenced after we're done.
  • Online or in-person? +
    Both. Online sessions happen over Google Meet. In-person is available in Delhi. Just mention your preference when you book.
